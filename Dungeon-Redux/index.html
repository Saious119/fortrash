Welcome to Dungeon Redux!

Welcome to Dungeon Redux, a rogue like RPG made in C#. In this game you'll create your character and head off on a 7 day jounrey into a cave (which may or may not lead to Hell). In this Rogue-like you'll have to manage your resources like stamina, hunger, and weapons. Fight against a variety of enemies, like "Suspicious Rock", and Bear, and Demon Boar.


Goals:

Creat a fully playable game with begining middle and end

Player Names for scores and high scores

A wide variety of enemies to fight

Weapons and inventory system

EXP and Level Up system

Magic System

Attribute points to give to stats at every level

Keep track of high scores after stopping the program

Save progress option

General UI improvements

Art (Pixel Art)

Music (chip tune)


You can grab the latest binary release for your system (Window 10, MacOS, and Linux) under the Releases page by clicking on the github logo below!
